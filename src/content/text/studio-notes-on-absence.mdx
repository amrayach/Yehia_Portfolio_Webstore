## Frame

Absence is not emptiness. It is a field that gives edges their weight. In monochrome work, this relationship becomes direct because color cannot distract from spacing.

In practice, I block the composition with generous margins first. The center appears later, after the perimeter is convincing.

## Rhythm

I build pages like sequences, not posters. One strong sentence, one pause, one turn. Repetition is useful only when the interval changes.

If a layout reads too quickly, I add air. If it reads too slowly, I remove texture.
