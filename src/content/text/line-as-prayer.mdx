## Opening

Every mark is a decision, but not every decision should be loud. I return to short gestures and repeated angles because they reduce noise and make intention visible.

When the line is simple, the eye cannot hide. It either follows or it leaves. This is useful pressure for studio work, where clarity matters more than volume.

## Practice

I keep one rule in every sketch: remove one element before adding another. That subtraction keeps the surface open and prevents decoration from replacing structure.

The page should feel measured, not crowded. White space is part of the sentence.
